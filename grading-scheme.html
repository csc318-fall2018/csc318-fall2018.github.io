Participation grade

The participation grade encompasses:
  • attendence to the lectures and tutorials
  • in-class activities hands-in
  • participation to the classes
  • group participation (peer-reviewed)

 

Assignments grade

The break-down of how each assignment will be graded is details in the assignment handouts.

Final presentation grade

The final presentations in tutorials will be graded according to the following criteria:
  • Topic & Idea Originality: Is this a brand new idea or a significant improvement over existing devices or technologies? Does the project address a new culture or service not currently sufficiently addressed by technology OR applies a significantly novel approach?
  • User Centeredness: Does the design address a real user need? Has the project responded to real user needs as uncovered by the research? Has the project used feedback from their users to improve the design?
  • Design Quality: Is the design well conveyed in prototypes and mockups? Are they using appropriate technologies for the defined problem? Does it match user needs and expectations, and match users' technical capabilities? Presentation
  • Quality: Audience engagement, presenter enthusiasm, delivery clarity, quality of presentation materials & timeliness.
